dreamOn

DreamOn is a wearable device that emits a gentle pulse to help people fall asleep.

WEBSITE
PITCH DECK

The Device

The device itself is 3D printed.
The hardware communicates with the mobile app via a bluetooth connection.

The App

The app consists of sleep tracking, guided meditations and breathing sessions, sleep stories, and the ability to change the device's pulse settings. The user logs into the app with their email and password, and their information is stored securely within that account. The app is compatible with apple and android products and free for use.

The Process

We consulted with numerous sleep psycologists and neurologist to understand the needs of sleep deprived people. My role was to lead user testing sessions once every three months with a wide variety of individuals.
