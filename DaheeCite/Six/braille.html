DESIGNER STATEMENT

This is my designer statement
translated into braille and lasercut into wood.

INCLUSIVITY

Inclusivity is a core value of mine.
Through this translation, I wanted to highlight that
anyone can make design accessible.

PROCESS

It was not a simple process to create this.
To create the lasercut file, I worked in Adobe Illustrator to create the vector file. To my shock, there was no standard braille font embedded into the program.
Through trial and error, I used an online third party translator to create a digital translation,
then verified the translation by asking
individuals who identified with blindness to read my statement out loud.

With this manifestation, I am not only demonstrating inclusive design, but I am also making a statement.
This is what inclusivity in design looks like.
As a designer, I will not accept design that does not include everyone.
Design is not good unless it encompasses all.
